Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed version of your password. We also store the brand names, queries, and AI platform API keys you provide to use the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and provide the Trackly service
  • To send queries to AI platforms on your behalf using the API keys you provide
  • To send transactional emails (verification, password reset)
  • To improve the service and fix bugs

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database. API keys are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. We use HTTPS for all connections.

4. Third-Party Services

When you run queries, Trackly sends requests to the AI platforms you have configured (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Perplexity). Your brand name and queries are sent to these platforms according to their respective privacy policies. We do not sell or share your data with any other third parties.

5. Your API Keys

API keys you provide are used solely to make requests on your behalf. They are encrypted at rest and never exposed in logs or to other users.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the settings page.

7. Cookies

Trackly uses a JWT token stored in your browser's local storage to maintain your session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at hello@trackly.so for any privacy-related requests.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, email us at hello@trackly.so.
