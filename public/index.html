FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI visibility tracking.

What is AI visibility tracking?

AI visibility tracking monitors how AI platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Grok, and Google AI Overview mention your brand when users ask questions. It helps you understand your brand's presence in AI-generated answers and optimize your visibility in generative search engines.

How does Trackly track brand mentions in AI?

Trackly sends your custom queries to real AI platforms via their official APIs and captures the complete, unmodified responses. It then analyzes each response to detect if your brand was mentioned, the sentiment, and whether your brand was recommended. All responses are saved as verifiable proof.

Which AI platforms does Trackly support?

Trackly tracks your brand across 8 AI platforms: ChatGPT (OpenAI), Perplexity AI, Claude (Anthropic), Google Gemini, Grok (xAI), Google AI Overview (AIO), DeepSeek, and Mistral. All queries use real API calls for authentic, verifiable results.

What is Share of Voice in AI?

Share of Voice (SOV) in AI measures what percentage of AI-generated responses mention your brand when relevant queries are asked. A higher SOV means AI platforms are more likely to recommend your brand to users, leading to more organic visibility and trust.

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing your brand's online presence to appear more frequently and positively in AI-generated answers. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on search engine rankings, GEO focuses on being mentioned and recommended by AI chatbots and assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google Gemini.

Can I use Trackly for client reporting?

Yes. Trackly saves complete AI responses as proof, which you can export as CSV reports. The Evidence & Proof section shows full AI responses with brand name highlighted, model used, and timestamp — perfect for client presentations and GEO audits.

Is Trackly free to use?

Yes, Trackly offers a free plan that includes 1 brand, 3 AI platforms, 5 queries, and evidence export. Pro ($29/mo) adds 5 brands, all 8 AI platforms, 25 queries, competitor tracking, and scheduled runs. Agency ($79/mo) unlocks 20 brands, 50 queries, and full access.

How is Trackly different from traditional SEO tools?

Traditional SEO tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, and Moz track your rankings on Google Search. Trackly tracks your visibility in AI-generated answers — a completely different channel. As more users shift to AI for recommendations, tracking your AI visibility becomes essential alongside traditional SEO.
