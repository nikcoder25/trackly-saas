# Domain Name Suggestions for Trackly SaaS

## About Trackly
Trackly is an **AI Visibility Tracker** — a SaaS platform that helps brands monitor how they appear across 8 major AI platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Google AIO, DeepSeek, Mistral). It tracks brand mentions, sentiment, competitor positioning, and Share of Voice in AI-generated answers.

---

## Tier 1 — Direct Brand Match (Best for brand recall)

| Domain | Why It Works |
|--------|-------------|
| `trackly.ai` | Perfect match — `.ai` TLD aligns with the AI-focused product |
| `trackly.io` | Classic SaaS TLD, developer/startup friendly |
| `trackly.co` | Short, clean, startup feel |
| `gettrackly.com` | Standard SaaS pattern if `trackly.com` is taken |
| `usetrackly.com` | Another common SaaS naming pattern |

## Tier 2 — Descriptive & Keyword-Rich (Great for SEO)

| Domain | Why It Works |
|--------|-------------|
| `aivisibility.com` | Describes exactly what the product does |
| `aibrandtracker.com` | Keyword-rich, self-explanatory |
| `aimentions.com` | Short, catchy, describes mention tracking |
| `brandpulse.ai` | Evokes real-time monitoring + AI TLD |
| `aishareofvoice.com` | Targets the SOV metric tracked by the platform |

## Tier 3 — Creative & Brandable (Memorable, unique)

| Domain | Why It Works |
|--------|-------------|
| `visiblyai.com` | "Visible" + AI — captures the core promise |
| `mentionly.ai` | Follows the "-ly" naming trend, mentions are the core feature |
| `rankdinai.com` | "Ranked in AI" — speaks to list position detection |
| `aisignal.co` | Short, implies brand signals across AI platforms |
| `echotrack.ai` | Brand's "echo" across AI platforms |

## Tier 4 — Premium / Authority Domains

| Domain | Why It Works |
|--------|-------------|
| `aivoice.io` | "Share of Voice" in AI — short and powerful |
| `sentinelai.co` | Implies watchful monitoring of AI platforms |
| `airadar.io` | Radar = scanning/tracking, very intuitive |
| `geotracker.ai` | Ties into GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) |

---

## Top 3 Recommendations

1. **`trackly.ai`** — Brand name + `.ai` TLD = perfect match for an AI visibility product
2. **`aivisibility.com`** — Strongest SEO play; people search "AI visibility" to find tools like this
3. **`brandpulse.ai`** — Premium feel, memorable, implies real-time monitoring

---

## Tips

- Avoid hyphens — they hurt brand recall
- Prioritize `.ai` or `.com` TLDs for highest trust signals
- Check trademark availability on USPTO before committing
- Secure matching social handles (Twitter/X, LinkedIn) simultaneously
